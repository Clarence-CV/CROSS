Abstract

Overview

Remote sensing referring segmentation requires a model to identify and segment a target region conditioned on natural language expressions. The task is challenging because of large-scale scenes, fine-grained object relations, sparse targets, and cross-modal alignment difficulty.

CROSS introduces a cascaded distillation pipeline together with dual constraints for grounding-aware representation learning, enabling stronger language-region alignment and more reliable segmentation in complex aerial imagery.
